Update #10, Friday April 3, 2020: All Stores and Online Ordering Open, With Restrictions
Posted by Tessa Violet on Apr 3rd 2020
As the pandemic continues to worsen in the U.S., we are implementing the following procedures as of today, Friday, April 3, 2020: Retail Stores: No handling of firearms by customers. This could be a spread point for the virus. Ranges are closed. No customers allowed with coughing, fever, or sneezing. Please consider wearing a mask. Limited customers in the store. We can only allow up to 10 customers in the store at a time. There may be lines outside.
